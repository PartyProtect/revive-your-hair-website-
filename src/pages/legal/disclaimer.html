    • International: findahelpline.com

10. Women and Pregnancy Warning

⚠️ CRITICAL WARNING FOR WOMEN: Finasteride is CONTRAINDICATED in women of childbearing potential. It causes severe birth defects in male fetuses. Pregnant women should not even touch broken or crushed finasteride tablets as the medication can be absorbed through skin.

Women who are or may become pregnant:

  • Must not take finasteride under any circumstances
  • Should not handle broken or crushed finasteride tablets
  • Should consult a healthcare provider about alternative treatments
  • Must use alternative hair loss treatments designed for women
