This Cookie Policy explains how Tomann & Alberts Enterprises (trading as ReviveYourHair) uses cookies and similar technologies on reviveyourhair.eu. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy for details on how we process personal data.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They enable core site features, help us improve performance, and—if you agree—allow us to personalize marketing.

2. How We Obtain Consent

On your first visit we display a cookie consent banner powered by CookieYes.

  • Strictly necessary cookies load automatically because they are required to deliver the service you expressly request (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).
  • Analytics, preference and marketing cookies are set only after you click "Accept all" or toggle them on in the banner (Art. 6(1)(a) consent).
  • You can withdraw or change consent at any time via the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer. Consent records are kept for 12 months, after which we will re-ask.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly necessary — Make the Site work: remember basket contents, process payments, secure log-ins. (Contract, Art. 6(1)(b))
  • Preference — Remember language, cookie choices, Elementor layout cache. (Legitimate interest, Art. 6(1)(f))
  • Analytics & performance — Count visits, see which pages are popular, fix errors. (Consent, Art. 6(1)(a))
  • Marketing — Send you relevant offers in newsletters or remarketing ads. (Consent, Art. 6(1)(a))

5. Third-Country Data Transfers

Some providers (e.g. Google, PayPal, MailerLite) are based in the United States. Data associated with their cookies may therefore be transferred outside the EEA. We rely on the EU-US Data Privacy Framework or Standard Contractual Clauses to safeguard such transfers (GDPR Arts. 44-49).

Google Analytics is configured with IP anonymization enabled so that your full IP address is never stored.

6. How to Manage Cookies

  • Banner controls: Click "Cookie Settings" in the footer to revisit our banner at any time.
  • Browser settings: Most browsers let you block or delete cookies site-by-site. See aboutcookies.org for guides.
  • GA opt-out add-on: Google offers a browser plug-in at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
Important:

Blocking strictly necessary cookies may make parts of the Site unusable (e.g. checkout).
