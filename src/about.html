How We Work

We start with peer-reviewed research
PubMed, medical journals, clinical trials. If it's not published and peer-reviewed, we don't cite it.
We look at sample sizes and methodology
A study with 12 people isn't the same as one with 1,200. We note the difference.
We translate medical language into plain English
"Androgenetic alopecia" becomes "pattern hair loss." "Follicular miniaturization" becomes "your hair is shrinking."
We explain the mechanisms, not just the treatments
Understanding why something works helps you make better decisions about your situation.
We're honest about limitations
Side effects happen. Not everything works for everyone. Some questions don't have good answers yet. We tell you.
